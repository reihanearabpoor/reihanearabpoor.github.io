I'm Reihane Arabpoor, a Ph.D. student in Industrial Engineering at the University of Illinois Chicago, where I am a member of the [Smarture Lab](https://smarture.lab.uic.edu/) under the supervision of [Dr. Azadeh Haghighi](https://smarture.lab.uic.edu/about.html). I received my B.Sc in Mechanical Engineering from Sharif University of Technology. My research spans **Additive Manufacturing**, **3D Printed Electronics**, **Aerosol Jet Printing**, **Multiphysics Simulations**, and **Machine Learning for Manufacturing**.
