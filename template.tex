    \resumeItemListStart
        \item Conducting research under the supervision of Dr. Azadeh Haghighi in additive manufacturing, printed electronics, and intelligent process optimization.
        
        \item \textbf{Investigation of Interfacial Fluid Mechanics and Complex Surface Printing in Aerosol Jet Printing (AJP):}
        Advanced the understanding of AJP for electronics manufacturing on complex and non-planar surfaces. Combined experimental studies, COMSOL Multiphysics simulations, and machine learningâ€“based models to enhance print quality, predictability, and process design. Characterized printed samples using high-resolution microscopy and electrical measurements to establish a framework for predictive design and process optimization, enabling conformal printing of electronics with high precision and repeatability.
        
        \item \textbf{Thermal Analysis of Complex Trajectories in Metal Additive Manufacturing:}
        Developed Physics-Informed Neural Network (PINN) models for predicting thermal histories across complex toolpaths in metal AM. The models demonstrated transferability across multi-track and multi-head laser systems, providing high-fidelity thermal predictions that support preprocessing and process optimization.
        
        \item \textbf{Synchronous Multi-Layer 3D Printing for Enhanced Structural Integrity:}
        Pioneered a novel synchronous multi-robot, multi-layer printing framework designed to improve structural strength and reduce fabrication time. The method was validated through analytical modeling, finite element simulations, and experimental testing, showing significant potential for scalable multi-material 3D printing.
        
        \item Performed multiphysics simulations in ANSYS Fluent to validate and benchmark thermal and fluid models under various additive manufacturing process conditions.
